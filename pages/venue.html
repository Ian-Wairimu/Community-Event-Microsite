Getting Here

By Public Transportation

The Metropolitan Convention Center is easily accessible via multiple public transportation options:

  • Subway: Central Station (Red & Blue Lines) - 5 minute walk
  • Bus: Routes 101, 205, 307 stop directly outside
  • Light Rail: Tech District Line - Convention Center Station

By Car

From downtown: Take Innovation Drive exit from Highway 101
Parking: Complimentary parking in the underground garage

Rideshare

Use drop-off point at Main Entrance on Innovation Drive
